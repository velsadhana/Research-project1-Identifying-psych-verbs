Psych verbs are emotional verbs which represent only emotions and no physical movements involved.
‘To be happy’, ‘to fear’, ‘to like’ are some examples for psych verbs.
For example:
(1) She was deeply moved by that. In this sentence, 'moved' is a psych verb as it denotes only emotional meaning.
(2) I moved the chair. In this sentence,'moved' is a not a psych verb as it doesn't denote emotion but only the physical movement.

This app has been developed for German language.
When you give a German sentence with any one of these verbs and the position of that verb in the sentence as inputs,
then the system will identify whether the respective verb is a psych verb or not.
