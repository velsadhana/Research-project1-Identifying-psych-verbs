Psych verbs are emotional verbs which represent only emotions and no physical movements involved.
‘To be happy’, ‘to fear’, ‘to like’ are some examples for psych verbs.
For example:
(1) I like it. In this sentence, 'like' is a psych verb as it represents an emotion meaning.
(2) I moved the chair. In this sentence,'moved' is a non-psych verb as there is no emotion but only the physical movement.

This app has been developed for German language.
When you give any German sentence with a verb and the position of verb in that sentence as inputs,
then the system will identify whether the respective verb is a psych verb or not.
